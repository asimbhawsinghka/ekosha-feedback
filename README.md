# eKosha — Feedback & Bug Reports

This is the public issue tracker for [eKosha](https://ekosha.co.in) — a secure platform for managing and sharing personal assets with family and trusted contacts.

## How to report a bug

1. Search [existing issues](../../issues) to avoid duplicates
2. Click **New Issue** → select **Bug Report**
3. Fill in all sections — the more detail, the faster we can fix it

## How to request a feature

1. Search [existing issues](../../issues) — someone may have asked already
2. Click **New Issue** → select **Feature Request**
3. Describe what you want and why it would help you

## What to include in a bug report

- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- Steps to reproduce
- Your Android version and device model
- Screenshots if relevant

## Response times

We aim to respond to all issues within 3 business days. Critical bugs affecting data safety will be prioritised.

## Contact

For account or billing issues, email [support@ekosha.co.in](mailto:support@ekosha.co.in).  
For privacy questions, email [privacy@ekosha.co.in](mailto:privacy@ekosha.co.in).
